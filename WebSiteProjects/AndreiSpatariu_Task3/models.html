Models

Raptor

Raptor is our most popular model,
a very versatile instrument of classic design.
With wide array of options available it may become
what any player seeks for himself.

Nebelung

It uses the Raptor model and sharing its versatility.
More aggressive appearance isn't the only difference.
Nebelung borrows some ergonomic features from Swan and
Shoggie models and both it's top and back are radius curved.
Only selected wood species are used as body and neck
material to ensure balanced sound.

Swan

Prioritizing comfort and tone it's one of our most ergonomic designs
designed with multi-scaled instruments in mind. Swan has radius curved
top with ergonomic contours. We don't offer chambering for this model.

Tamandua

This is how we imagine a contemporary Tele. Tamandua features a resonant
chamber under an inlaid pickguard. Wood choices for this model were made
with particular tone goal we had in mind.

chiRoptera

Chiroptera combines aggressive look with comfort and excellent balance.
